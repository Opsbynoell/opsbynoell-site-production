## Non-Negotiables
- No coral.
- Warm neutrals, blush/pink family, soft lilac, and wine/oxblood accents.
- Purple is reserved only for Noell-specific moments.
- The site must feel like one authored world.
- Do not redesign from scratch; use the existing template/base.
- Keep full architecture: Home, Systems, Verticals, Pricing, Noell, About, Book.
- Keep conversion logic intact.
- Keep urgency, pressure, operational specificity, and proof.

## Strategic Architecture
- Use `Verticals` as the architecture and route system.
- Keep the hub at `/verticals`.
- Preserve the future landing-page model under `/verticals/...`.
- The homepage should route; it should not try to carry all detail.

## Core Brand Thesis
- Ops by Noell lives in the space between cold corporate automation and warm human done-for-you relief.
- The site should make modern AI feel familiar and human.
- The core bridge is between old communication systems and modern AI systems.
- Presence, contact lists, status indicators, browser windows, and layered interfaces are useful cultural cues when used with restraint.

## Homepage Psychology Sequence
1. Recognition
2. Tension
3. Proof
4. Relief
5. Action

### Meaning
- Recognition: show the visitor their problem before explaining the solution.
- Tension: make the invisible leak feel urgent and expensive.
- Proof: show operational evidence before feature detail.
- Relief: show what life feels like when the system carries the load.
- Action: make the audit / booking step feel obvious and low-friction.

## Live Site Elements To Preserve
- Hero thesis: “By the time you call back, they've already booked somewhere else.”
- Dual CTA logic.
- Proof band: recovered revenue, reviews, no-show reduction, speed to results.
- Dark interruption: “Your marketing is working. Your response time isn't.”
- Case-study logic.
- About/founder trust logic.
- Closing audit CTA.
- Site-wide Noell assistant logic.

## What To Upgrade
- Make the site feel less like polished generic SaaS and more like an authored campaign world.
- Increase hierarchy, composition, and editorial tension.
- Keep proof, but make it feel like operational evidence artifacts rather than generic cards.
- Reduce template-default sameness.
- Use fewer but stronger visual ideas.

## Noell Rules
- Noell gets its own page.
- Noell must be visible and testable in preview.
- Purple belongs to Noell only.
- Build a branded launcher, panel states, and mock conversation flow if live backend is not wired yet.
- Noell should feel integrated into the same premium world, not like a separate startup product.

## `/book` Rules
- `/book` must be a real branded booking experience, not a stub.
- Include reassurance copy, trust microcopy, expectation-setting, and a polished GHL/Calendly embed scaffold.
- This page is the Action stage of the homepage psychology.

## Component Discipline
- Use as few advanced components as possible.
- Do not stack trendy motion components.
- Premium framed proof objects are allowed if they feel authored.
- Use advanced components only if they improve hierarchy, proof clarity, or emotional impact.

## Final Test
If the logo disappeared, the site should still feel unmistakably like Ops by Noell.
It should feel quiet, precise, memorable, authored, and conversion-aware.
